Health & Hygiene are very important to prevent the spread of Diseases.

Some important points to stay healthy are :
1. We should wash our hands frequently with soap.
2. It is also important is to get a vaccine for those infections and viruses.
3. Use a tissue, or cough and sneeze into your arm, not your hand and turn away from other people.
4. Do not touch your eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.
5. Wash your hands after coughing, sneezing or using tissues.
